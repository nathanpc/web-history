The Web in Eras of Design

The history of the web can be sub-divided into multiple eras, each one representing a certain time and having its distinct asthetic.

  • 1980-1998 The internet's humble beginnings
  • 1999-2003 Design meets the web.
  • 2004-2007 Gradients everywhere.
  • 2008-2011 The "modern" and skeumorphic era of design on the web and the mobile revolution.
  • 2012-2016 Bootstrap everywhere!
  • 2017-Today Material and flat design
